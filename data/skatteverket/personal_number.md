# Skatteverket (Swedish Tax Agency) — Personal Number (Personnummer)

## Source
- URL: https://www.skatteverket.se/english
- Agency: Skatteverket (Swedish Tax Agency)
- Last Updated: 2026-03-22

## Overview

When you move to Sweden, you may receive a personal identity number (personnummer). This is a unique number assigned by the Swedish Tax Agency. The personal number is essential for living in Sweden — you need it for healthcare, banking, insurance, employment, and most government services.

## Who Needs a Personal Number

- Anyone who plans to live in Sweden for one year or more
- EU/EEA citizens who have the right of residence
- Non-EU citizens with a residence permit for at least one year
- Asylum seekers (after receiving residence permit)

## Eligibility

To get a personal number you must:
1. Be registered in the Swedish population register (folkbokföring)
2. Intend to stay in Sweden for at least one year
3. Have the right to live in Sweden (work permit, residence permit, or EU right of residence)

## Steps to Get a Personal Number

1. **Move to Sweden** — You must physically be in Sweden
2. **Visit a Skatteverket office** — Bring your passport, residence permit/work permit, and proof of address
3. **Fill in the registration form** — SKV 7632 (Moving to Sweden)
4. **Bring supporting documents**:
   - Valid passport or national ID card (EU citizens)
   - Residence permit or proof of right of residence
   - Marriage certificate (if applicable, apostilled or legalized)
   - Birth certificates for children
   - Employment contract or proof of studies
5. **Submit in person** — Both you and your family members must visit the office in person
6. **Wait for processing** — Usually takes 2-4 weeks
7. **Receive your personal number** — You will receive a letter with your personal number

## Important Notes

- You cannot apply for a personal number from abroad — you must be in Sweden
- The personal number format is: YYYYMMDD-XXXX
- If you stay less than one year, you get a coordination number (samordningsnummer) instead
- ID cards can be applied for after you receive your personal number
- Free of charge
